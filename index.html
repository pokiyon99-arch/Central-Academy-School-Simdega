Welcome to Central Academy School

Established in 2012 by our visionary Director and Principal Janki Sinha, Central Academy School has been a beacon of quality education in Simdega. We are committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for a bright future.

As an English medium institution, we provide comprehensive education from Nursery to Class 10, focusing on academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth of every student.
