Welcome to Central Academy School

Established in 2012 by our visionary Director and Principal Janki Sinha, Central Academy School has been a beacon of quality education in Simdega. We are committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for a bright future.

As an English medium institution, we provide comprehensive education from Nursery to Class 10, focusing on academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth of every student.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our educational excellence

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, fosters creativity, and develops strong moral values. We aim to create a learning environment where every child can achieve their full potential and become a responsible global citizen.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Simdega, known for academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and holistic development of students. We envision creating future leaders who will contribute positively to society.
