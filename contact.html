Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is the admission process?

Our admission process includes filling out an application form, submitting required documents, and attending an interaction session. Please contact us for detailed information.

What are the school fees?

Our fee structure varies by class and includes tuition, activities, and other charges. Please call us or visit the school for current fee details.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation services to various areas in Simdega. Contact us to check if we cover your area.

What curriculum do you follow?

We follow the CBSE curriculum with additional focus on co-curricular activities, ensuring holistic development of every student.
